Bryan P Brown


Howdy, I'm Bryan, a Postdoctoral Fellow @ Seattle Children's Research Institute where I study bacterial-viral interactions in enteric human communities. I'm interested in building statistical tools to robustly analyze and integrate multiomic datasets to delineate the crosstalk between our immune system and the cohort of bacteria and viruses that colonize our enteric niches.

If I'm not at the bench or desk, there's a good shot that I'm fighting my way up a beautiful cliff or thinking too much about brewing that perfect cup of joe.
